# LootCheck Brand Guidelines

The LootCheck name, logo, icon artwork, banners, and store listing identity are project branding. They are not licensed for confusing or misleading use.

You may use the LootCheck name to truthfully refer to this project, link to the official repository, or describe compatibility with LootCheck.

You may not use the LootCheck name, logo, icon artwork, banners, or confusingly similar branding for a fork, redistributed build, store listing, paid service, or commercial product without written permission.

Forks and redistributed builds should use a different name and clearly state that they are not the official LootCheck project.
